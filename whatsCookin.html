What’s Cookin’ is a website that aims to merge a social media experience with a recipe focus. My two other classmates and I built this in a little over a week. This website uses ASP. Net, Identity, C#, LINQ, Google Authentication, SQL, repository design pattern, HTML, and CSS. You can do several functions through this application like make your own recipe, rate and save others recipes and even suggest edits to others recipes to enhance what they already have.
